What is PEAK?

PEAK is a training program designed to help new insurance agents come sprinting out of the gates. Our name, PEAK, is an acronym for the core values of our program.

Purpose

This pillar is essential to be great in anything you do in life…know your WHY.

Effort

Accountability

This pillar is the roadmap to the top of the mountain.

Knowledge

This pillar separates the great from the good. Always be learning & advancing.

Base Camp

PEAK Base Camp is the launching pad for our 90-day program. We'll meet together for intensive training sessions over the course of two days which will equip and prepare every agent for the remainder of our 90-day program.

The Base Camp investment provides two days of classroom training, lunches for both days, and the 90-day Base Camp manual for the Agent. Team Member lodging and transportation not included.
